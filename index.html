Warning.. This game involves addition.

It is an ordeal of prime numbers! Bubble will keep falling from the sky. Your task is to clear ball as quickly as you can. You have seven turns to clear ball before more hidden ball fall from the sky!

    Controls:
  • Use ← and → key to control the movement
  • Use spacebar to drop ball quickly
    To clear ball:
  1. Balls can be clear if a series of two or more balls next to each other sum up to a prime.
  2. When there is a hidden ball in the column, sum of prime in columns cannot cancel out .
