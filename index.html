Instructions

It is an ordeal of prime numbers! Bubble will keep falling from the sky. Your task is to clear balls as quickly as you can. You have seven turns to clear balls before more hidden balls fall from the sky!

    Controls:
  • Use ← and → key to control the movement
  • Use spacebar to drop ball quickly
    Rules:
  1. All the balls connected to each other sum up to a prime will be cleared
  2. - At least two or more balls must be in the chain
  3. When there is a hidden ball in a column, sum of prime in columns cannot be cleared
  4. - Hidden ball has no effect on a row
  5. Hidden ball can be revealed if any of its surrounding balls get cleared
